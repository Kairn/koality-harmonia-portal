About Us

Our Mission

Koality Harmonia strives to become the most open platform for people to share music online. We believe in free music and encourage music creators to publish their digital albums without charge. The content on our site will never be censored at any level, and we allow users themselves to decide what to consume.

DisclaimerIII

Our application is still in the early stage of development. We make great effort to maintain our server and database, but we cannot guarantee that all services will function without issues. Also, there is no content curation on our site, and Koality Harmonia is not responsible for any uploaded content that is in violation of intellectual property laws or otherwise illegal.

FAQs

Q1: III Why do I need to register for an account?

You need an account so we can save your information and store your content in the database. Only active participants can enjoy all the functionalities of our site. Don't worry, we respect our users' privacy and will never share your personal data with any third party.

Q2: III How do I upload and share my music?

First, you need to login to your account and navigate to the "Manage Album" section. Then, click on the "Create New Album" button and enter some basic information. Once an album is created, you can add tracks to the selected album. When you are done uploading all tracks, click on the "Publish" button to share it. You can optionally set a price (in ETA coins) so you get rewarded for each account's access. Additionally, you can have demo tracks in an album which are publicly playable by anyone with an account.

Q3: III How can I get access to music albums published by others?

You can visit the "Koality Store" after you login to view all published albums. Some albums will be free, and others will require you to pay a certain amount of ETA coins to get access. Once you own the album, navigate to the "Inventory" section and select the desired album. You will be redirected to the album page where you can play individual tracks by simply clicking on their titles.
Q4: III What are ETA coins and how can I earn it?

ETA coins are virtual currencies used in our application. They are like social credits and are not exchangeable for real currencies. Each account starts with 100 ETA coins, and you earn them by posting daily moments, publishing albums, and writing album reviews.
Q5: III How can I raise an issue or contribute to the project?

Our project is free and open-source. You need to reach out to the system administrator who deploys the app to raise an issue. You can also contact the original creator Kairn on GitHub for additional help or to offer your support.
